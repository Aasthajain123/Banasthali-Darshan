HOSTELS


Banasthali Vidyapith

                                               is a residential institution. A student admitted to 
                                              University will compulsorily have to join a hostel 
                                               unless student is ward of a worker of University and
                                              stays with him/her in the campus or the student belongs
                                               in a neighbouring village community within a radius of 
                                                2.5 Km. There are 31 student hostels in campus of the
                                               University. There is different number of rooms in each

                                             with basic amenities provided. Each hostel has a separate
                                                      Mess,Newspaper and Telephone facility
