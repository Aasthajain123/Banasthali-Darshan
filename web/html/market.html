MARKETS AT CAMPUS



OLD MARKET





OLd Market is situated near school hostels
and Sarda Mandir .It is the oldest market
of Banasthali Vidyapith. It is the perfect
blend of chill mood and happiness.It has many
shops that satisfy all the needs of students
specially the school students.The students can
celebrate their birthday parties and all
their special days.It has shoes shop, bag repairing
shops,general store,gift shops and vegetable marts
